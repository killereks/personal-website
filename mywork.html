ReConnect
Status
Finished

A student network which promotes interaction within the University of Exeter's student community. This aims to provide social opportunities by encouraging students to engage with each other in a fun and friendly online environment.

Skills learned / Features:
Group project (6 people), we divided responsibilities based on our skillsets to make sure we finish the product on time.
Ability to post different types of content (text, video, image, quiz).
Find people with similar interests and hobbies.
Dynamic searching using AJAX.
I was responsible for the website design and some of the back end in posting content and searching users.
